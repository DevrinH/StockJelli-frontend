Key takeaways:

The 4-hour window was the sweet spot — highest average gain (+1.76%) with a solid 54% win rate and the best reward-to-risk ratio (avg win +4.32% vs avg loss -1.24%, which is 3.5:1). That's an exceptional asymmetry.

The 6-hour window had the highest win rate at 60% — meaning 6 out of 10 entries were profitable after 6 hours. The avg gain was lower (+0.79%) because the winners were smaller (+2.42%), but the consistency was the best of any interval.

Even the shortest interval (5 min) was positive: +0.92% average with a 51% win rate and a 3:1 avg win vs avg loss ratio (+2.63% vs -0.83%). This suggests the screener is catching entries right as they're starting to move, not after the move is done.

Compare this to the prior two weeks: W09 was negative short-term / positive long-term. W10 was positive short-term / negative long-term. This week was positive across the board. Three consecutive weeks, three completely different outcome profiles. This is exactly why we're collecting this data — the optimal strategy changes weekly, and the goal is to eventually detect which regime you're in and adjust accordingly.
