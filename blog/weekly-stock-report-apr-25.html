Push Notification Performance

This was the first full week of live push notifications. The system fired 26 stock alerts across the five trading days. Of those, 14 hit the +3% target from the alert price — a 54% hit rate for the week. Notably, all 26 alerts stayed positive from the push price (never went red), meaning every single alerted ticker continued moving up after the notification was sent, even if not all reached the +3% threshold.

Top-performing alerts included SKLZ (+89.6% from push), XNDU (+30.4%), TRT (+29.9%), and TORO (+18.6%). Several Tier 2 alerts — stocks that were flagged at 15 minutes and confirmed at 30 minutes — also performed well, with FCEL (+7.4%), NKTR (+6.7%), and AOSL (+3.3%) all clearing the target.

The highest-RVOL entries this week also produced some of the largest moves, reinforcing that unusual volume remains a key signal.

Entry timing was concentrated in the first 90 minutes of the trading day: 117 of 207 entries (57%) occurred between 9:00 and 9:59 AM ET, with another 32 between 10:00 and 10:59. Activity tapered sharply after the morning session.
