This is new. Starting this week, StockJelli tracks what happens to every screener entry at fixed time intervals after it's flagged. We measured 104 stock entries across five time windows:

What this tells us: Stock screener entries had a positive expected value across every time window this week. End-of-day was the strongest: average entry up +2.09% with a 57% win rate. Winners averaged +6.21% while losers averaged -3.32% — a nearly 2:1 reward-to-risk ratio.

The win/loss asymmetry is the key insight: average wins were consistently larger than average losses at every interval. This means even with a modest 52–57% win rate, the expected value is positive because winners run further than losers fall. That's the edge momentum trading is built on.

Note: This is one week of data (104 entries). We're publishing it for full transparency, not as a guarantee. The outcome tracking will become more meaningful as we accumulate weeks of data across different market conditions.
