Early observation: the entries with the highest peak gains didn't necessarily have the highest RVOL. BATL was the top performer (+144%) at RVOL 2.78x, while ANNA had the highest RVOL (6.28x) but a more modest peak (+39.1%). More weeks of data are needed, but the initial signal is that RVOL above 2x seems to be a meaningful threshold — every top 10 entry this week cleared it except GDXD (0.83x), which also had the smallest peak gain of the group.
