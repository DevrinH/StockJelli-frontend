About

Clean momentum. No noise.

What is StockJelli?

StockJelli is a real-time momentum screener built for traders who want to catch moves early. We scan the market every minute to surface stocks and crypto showing unusual strength — the kind of clean breakouts that often lead to bigger runs.

No cluttered dashboards. No lagging indicators. Just a focused list of what's moving right now, with the context you need to decide if it's worth a closer look.

How It Works

We filter thousands of assets down to the ones that matter using a combination of:

  • % Change — Only stocks up 4%+ and crypto up 3%+ make the list
  • Volume — Minimum 1M in trading volume ensures liquidity
  • Market Cap — Filters out shell companies and mega-caps that rarely move big
  • News — When available, we link to recent coverage from trusted sources
  • Range Position — See where price sits within the day's high-low range

The result is a clean, scannable list that updates every 60 seconds during market hours.

Features

  • Stocks & Crypto — Toggle between equities and digital assets
  • Live Indices — NASDAQ, S&P 500, BTC, and total crypto market at a glance
  • Smart Filters — Adjust market cap, price, volume, and news requirements
  • TradingView Links — One click to open any ticker in TradingView

Data Sources

Stock and crypto price data powered by Tiingo. Crypto market data enriched by CoinGecko. News headlines from Finnhub. All data is provided for informational purposes and may be delayed or incomplete.

What StockJelli Is Not

StockJelli is not financial advice, not a signal service, and not a guarantee of returns. Momentum trading carries significant risk. Always do your own research and never trade with money you can't afford to lose.

Built By

StockJelli is an independent project built by a solo developer who got tired of noisy trading platforms and wanted something cleaner. Questions or feedback? Use the Contact form — we actually read every message.
