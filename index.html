What is StockJelli?

StockJelli is a real-time momentum screener built for traders who want to catch moves early. We scan the market every minute to surface stocks and crypto showing unusual strength ‚Äî the kind of clean breakouts that often lead to bigger runs.

No cluttered dashboards. No lagging indicators. Just a focused list of what's moving right now, with the context you need to decide if it's worth a closer look.

How It Works

We filter thousands of assets down to the ones that matter using a combination of:

  • % Change ‚Äî Only stocks up 4%+ and crypto up 3%+ make the list
  • Volume ‚Äî Minimum 1M in trading volume ensures liquidity
  • Market Cap ‚Äî Filters out shell companies and mega-caps that rarely move big
  • News ‚Äî When available, we link to recent coverage from trusted sources
  • Range Position ‚Äî See where price sits within the day's high-low range

The result is a clean, scannable list that updates every 60 seconds during market hours.
