🔔 How to Enable Push Notifications

Get real-time momentum alerts on your phone or desktop. Takes about 30 seconds.

🤖 Android (Chrome) Chrome required
  1. Open stockjelli.com in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (top right) → "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Open StockJelli from your home screen This makes it behave like a native app — full screen, no browser bar.
  4. Subscribe to notifications when prompted — you'll get native push alerts just like any other app
🍎 iOS (Safari) iOS 16.4+ · Safari only
  1. Open stockjelli.com in Safari Must be Safari — Chrome on iOS doesn't support web push notifications.
  2. Tap the Share button (bottom center, square with arrow) → "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Open StockJelli from your home screen This step is required — notifications only work from the home screen app, not from Safari directly.
  4. Subscribe when prompted — alerts will appear in your notification center and lock screen
💻 Chrome Desktop (Mac / Windows) Chrome, Edge, or Firefox
  1. Go to stockjelli.com in Chrome
  2. Click the subscribe / enable notifications prompt on the site
  3. Click "Allow" when Chrome asks for notification permission — alerts will pop up on your desktop in real time
Tip: For the best experience on mobile, add StockJelli to your home screen. This makes it open full-screen like a native app and ensures push notifications work reliably. You only need to do this once.

Not getting notifications?

Check your browser permissions: Go to your browser settings → Site Settings → Notifications → make sure stockjelli.com is set to "Allow."

iOS users: You must open StockJelli from the home screen icon, not from Safari. Also make sure you're on iOS 16.4 or later.

Do Not Disturb: If your phone is in DND or Focus mode, notifications may be silenced. Add StockJelli to your allowed apps.
