Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

Summary

StockJelli is an educational tool. We collect minimal data needed to operate the site and improve performance. We do not sell your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand site usage (e.g., pages visited, approximate location, device type).
  • Log data: Our servers may log request metadata (IP address, user agent, timestamps) for security and reliability.
  • Account & payments: If you subscribe to alerts, we collect your email address and payment status via our payment provider (Stripe). We do not store your credit card information directly.

How We Use Information

  • Operate and maintain the service.
  • Send you alert emails if you subscribe.
  • Monitor performance, prevent abuse, and debug issues.
  • Improve features and user experience.

Cookies

We and our partners (including advertising partners) may use cookies or similar technologies. Cookies help us understand how you use our site and allow our advertising partners to serve relevant ads.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

Advertising

We use Ezoic to provide personalized advertising on our site. Ezoic and its partners may collect and use data about your visits to this and other websites to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

To learn more about interest-based advertising or to opt out, visit:

  • Network Advertising Initiative
  • Digital Advertising Alliance
  • European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics — Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: Payment processing — Privacy Policy
  • Ezoic: Advertising — Privacy Policy
  • Amazon SES: Email delivery — Privacy Policy

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, unless a longer period is required by law. If you cancel your subscription, we may retain your email for record-keeping purposes but will stop sending you alerts.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access your personal data.
  • The right to correct inaccurate data.
  • The right to delete your data.
  • The right to opt out of certain data processing.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

Your Choices

  • You may block cookies in your browser settings.
  • You may use privacy tools (e.g., tracker blocking extensions).
  • You may opt out of interest-based advertising using the links above.
  • You may unsubscribe from alert emails at any time.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy
